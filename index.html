About Home Cook 101

Our Mission

Founded in 2020, our mission is to inspire budding home chefs to experiment with new recipes. We scour various sources - the internet, cook books, and even our families and friends - for noteworthy recipes that are simple to follow, and delicious to taste.

Our Recipes

From savory delights to sweet treats, Home Cook 101 publishes new recipes daily to keep things fresh. We cover cuisines to suit all tastebuds, so that you will never be bored. Besides, is there any better way to hone your culinery skills than to experiment with recipes from all around the world? We don't think so.

Our Audience

Whether you're eight or 80, home cooking can be fun and relaxing. All it takes is a suitable recipe and the openness to try. Whether you're a pro or just learning to cook, we provide just the right recipe to match your level of expertise. Here at Home Cook 101, we want to prove that anyone can cook.
